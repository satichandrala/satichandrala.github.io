About Me

About Me

I'm Purna Sati Chandrala, a passionate data scientist and geospatial expert based in Bonn, Germany. I hold a Master's degree in Geoinformation and Spatial Development from the University of Bonn.

With a background in Civil Engineering and extensive experience in machine learning, GIS, and data management, I bring a unique blend of skills to the table. My journey in the field started with a Bachelor's in Civil Engineering from JNTU Kakinada, India.

Throughout my academic and professional career, I have excelled in various roles, including Data Scientist at the University of Jena, Spatial Data Analyst at GESIS - Leibniz-Institut für Sozialwissenschaften, and Graduate Research Assistant at Ruhr-Universität Bochum. My work involves utilizing technologies such as OpenCV, Python, GIS libraries (ArcGIS, QGIS, GDAL), and machine learning frameworks (TensorFlow, Keras, PyTorch).
