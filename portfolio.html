About Me

I'm Purna Sati Chandrala, a passionate data scientist and geospatial expert based in Bonn, Germany. I hold a Master's degree in Geoinformation and Spatial Development from the University of Bonn.

With a background in Civil Engineering and extensive experience in machine learning, GIS, and data management, I bring a unique blend of skills to the table. My journey in the field started with a Bachelor's in Civil Engineering from JNTU Kakinada, India.

Throughout my academic and professional career, I have excelled in various roles, including a Data Scientist at the University of Jena, a Spatial Data Analyst at GESIS - Leibniz-Institut für Sozialwissenschaften, and a Graduate Research Assistant at Ruhr-Universität Bochum. My work involves utilizing technologies such as OpenCV, Python, GIS libraries (ArcGIS, QGIS, GDAL), and machine learning frameworks (TensorFlow, Keras, PyTorch).

Feel free to explore my portfolio to learn more about my projects and contributions. Connect with me on LinkedIn and GitHub for the latest updates.
